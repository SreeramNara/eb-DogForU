About DogForU

DOGFORU was built by a 13-year-old boy from Toronto. He has a great love for dogs and pets in general. His mission is to help you find a dog based on your needs. You can also access information about nearby animal shelters and adoption organizations in the U.S. and Canada. DOGFORU has over 260 different dog breeds for you to explore and has a lot of useful information about each dog, such as what it is bred for, its temperament and its life-span.
